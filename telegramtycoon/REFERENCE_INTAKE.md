## Visual Decisions

Farm screen direction:

4 chambers can borrow the same capsule material language, but Farm should remain lighter than Lab.

Plant direction:

Thin bio-light structures, glowing nodes, DNA/root logic, no chunky cartoon leaves.

Root energy direction:

Energy should rise from the base into the specimens like the DNA spiral energy in the reference.

Button direction:

Raised neon glass buttons with clear Stars price and compact toggle controls.

Capsule direction:

Heavy sci-fi top and base, glass center, cyan outlines, mechanical connectors.

Color palette:

Dark teal/green base, cyan glow, mint highlights, purple mutation accent, gold Stars.
