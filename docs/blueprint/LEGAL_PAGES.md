# Legal Pages

## Purpose
The product needs basic legal pages before any public launch.

Required pages:
- Terms and Conditions
- Privacy Policy

Current static pages:
- `terms.html`
- `privacy.html`

Later:
- Payment/Refund Policy
- Community Guidelines
- Cookie Policy if needed for web

## Settings Placement
Legal links should be available in:
- Account / More
- Settings
- Web footer later
- support flow if relevant

MVP menu labels:
- `Terms and Conditions`
- `Privacy Policy`

MVP URLs:
- `/terms.html`
- `/privacy.html`

## Terms And Conditions
Terms should explain:
- what the product is
- account rules
- acceptable use
- game/economy rules can change
- BioCards are digital collectibles in phase 1
- no NFT/TON ownership in phase 1
- Stars purchases are handled through Telegram rules
- refunds/support process
- limitations of liability
- termination/suspension
- contact/support route

## Privacy Policy
Privacy Policy should explain:
- what user data is collected
- Telegram identity data
- web identity data later
- wallet address if user links wallet later
- gameplay data
- payment metadata
- analytics/events
- support messages
- how data is stored
- how long data may be kept
- user rights/contact
- account deletion request flow
- what data may be retained for ledger, refund, audit, security, or legal reasons

## Important Product Rules
Do not make medical claims around Meditation.

Meditation can be framed as:
- relaxation
- focus
- calm ritual
- wellness-inspired feature

Avoid:
- treating illness
- curing anxiety
- medical outcomes
- guaranteed mental health effects

## MVP Build Recommendation
For first MVP:
- add `Terms and Conditions` link to `terms.html`
- add `Privacy Policy` link to `privacy.html`
- create simple static pages before external testing
- do not launch paid Stars flow publicly without legal review
- add account deletion section and link it from Settings
