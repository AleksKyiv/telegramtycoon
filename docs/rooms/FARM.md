# Farm Room Passport

## Role

Farm is the starter room and resource engine. It teaches the player the loop through visual growth, capsule slots, drone support, and first energy pressure.

## Current Elements

- Capsule chamber
- Plant chamber carousel
- One active product and locked/future slots
- Drone on the left side
- Energy / water / power indicators
- Auto collect
- Stars slot concept
- Bottom navigation

## Future Direction

Farm should become a premium microculture system, not a simple farm. It needs different plant structures, chamber scroll, slot unlocks, drone upgrades, and clear feedback when something grows or is ready.

## Stable Rules

- Bottom buttons must not move.
- Bottom navigation must not move.
- Main plant/capsule must stay readable on mobile.
- Stars payment buttons must be visually obvious when paid content is involved.
- Any paid slot unlock must be connected to server state before it becomes real.

## Data To Save

- Active plant state
- Slot ownership
- Resources
- Drone level
- Auto collect state
- Last collect time
- Farm actions history

## Admin Needs

- Opens of Farm
- Grow clicks
- Collects
- Auto collect enabled/disabled
- Slot unlock attempts
- Stars purchases tied to Farm
