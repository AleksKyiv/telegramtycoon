# Lab Room Passport

## Role

Lab is the mutation and artifact room. It receives Farm output and turns it into rare, exciting results that later influence Zen and future NFT logic.

## Current Elements

- Mutation screen inspired by biotech capsule reference
- Fusion process concept
- Mutation odds concept
- Stars confirm action concept
- Artifact result idea

## Future Direction

Lab should feel like a high-value reveal machine. The player should feel that a complex process is happening, then receive a rare result, artifact, or failed/partial mutation.

## Stable Rules

- Lab must not be treated as a simple button screen.
- Mutation results must be server-controlled when they matter.
- Randomness must be logged.
- Paid mutation boosts must use confirmed Stars payments only.

## Data To Save

- Mutation attempts
- Inputs consumed
- Result artifact
- Rarity
- Odds at the moment of mutation
- Payment boost used or not

## Admin Needs

- Mutation attempts by player
- Success/fail/result
- Rarity distribution
- Paid boost usage
- Artifact inventory history
