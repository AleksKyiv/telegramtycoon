Privacy Policy

Draft policy for the Green Farm Telegram Mini App prototype. Replace the contact and company details before public launch.

Last updated: May 24, 2026.

Data We Collect

  • Telegram profile data provided to the Mini App, such as Telegram ID, username, first name, language, and avatar when available.
  • Gameplay data, including score, energy, resonance, progress, rooms, drone level, skins, missions, and unlocked slots.
  • Payment records for Telegram Stars purchases, including product ID, amount, status, Telegram charge identifiers, and delivery status.
  • Basic technical logs needed to keep the app secure and working.

How We Use Data

  • To save player progress and restore it across sessions.
  • To deliver purchased digital goods and handle support requests.
  • To detect abuse, fix bugs, and improve gameplay balance.
  • To comply with platform, legal, tax, accounting, and security obligations.

Payments

Telegram Stars payments are processed by Telegram. We store only the records needed to verify purchase delivery, support, refunds, and accounting.

User Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. Contact us to request help with your data.
